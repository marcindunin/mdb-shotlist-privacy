Privacy Policy for MDB Shotlist

Last updated: December 2024

Overview

MDB Shotlist ("the App") is a companion application for film and video production crews. This privacy policy explains how the App handles your data.

Data Collection

The App does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data to external servers. Specifically:

  • No personal information is collected
  • No analytics or tracking is implemented
  • No data is shared with third parties
  • No advertisements are displayed

Local Data Storage

The App stores the following data locally on your device only:

  • Server connection settings (IP address and port)
  • Saved connection history (up to 5 connections)
  • View preferences (selected camera, settings)

This data never leaves your device and is only used to improve your experience with the App.

Network Communication

The App communicates only with shotlist servers that you explicitly configure. The App connects to:

  • Your specified server IP address and port
  • Only within your local network

No data is sent to any other servers or services.

Permissions

The App requires the following permissions:

  • Internet: To connect to your shotlist server on the local network
  • Network State: To check network connectivity status

Children's Privacy

The App does not collect any personal information from anyone, including children under 13 years of age.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated revision date.

Contact

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please contact us at: privacy@mdb-shotlist.app
